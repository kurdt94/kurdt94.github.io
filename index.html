Better-YT-Volume

A Basic Google Chrome extension that increases volume on youtube.com. Why ? Youtube normalizes audio to -14 LUFS integrated loudness.
Trust me, we need the extra volume.

Installation

    1. Download this repo as a ZIP file on your computer and unzip it to your folder of choice.

  • https://github.com/kurdt94/BetterYT-Volume/archive/refs/tags/stable.zip

    1. Go to chrome://extensions/ , and check the box for Developer mode in the top right.
    1. Click the Load unpacked extension button and select the unzipped folder to install.
